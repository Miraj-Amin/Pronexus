Section 1

Executive Summary

Walnut Marches is a consented residential development of 29 units at Station Road, East Grinstead (RH19). Full planning permission was granted November 2025. The scheme proposes 18 houses and 11 flats, all open market, with a gross development value of £14.5 million.

The appraisal produces a project profit of £3.25 million (22.4% on GDV), above our minimum threshold of 20%. Peak funding is estimated at £9.2 million on a 24-month programme, representing 63.4% of GDV — within lending parameters. The scheme is recommended to proceed to heads of terms with the proposed funder.
